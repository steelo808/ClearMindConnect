About CMC

Clear mind connect is dedicated to fostering dementia awareness, empathy, and support within communities. Our mission is to provide a compassionate platform that empowers individuals affected by dementia, their caregivers, and the wider community to understand, connect, and navigate the journey with clarity and compassion. Through education, resources, and advocacy, we aim to bridge gaps, reduce stigma, and promote a culture of inclusivity and understanding, ensuring that no one faces dementia alone.
